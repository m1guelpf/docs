If you have recently requested a deletion of a Vercel account within the last 48 hours and would like to reverse that decision, please contact Vercel Support immediately using the [support form](https://vercel.com/support/request) available from the Vercel dashboard. You can also directly email [support@vercel.com](mailto:support@vercel.com).

To expedite the process, please clearly include the slug or email associated with the requested Vercel account.
