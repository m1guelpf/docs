The Vercel platform is static first and backed by the [Vercel Edge Network](/edge-network), providing the highest levels of availability, reliability, and performance.

If there is any degradation in the service provided either via the Vercel dashboard or API, this information is made available on the [Vercel status page](https://vercel-status.com).

## Status Updates

All service degradation and issues impacting the platform will be reported on the following channels:

- [https://vercel-status.com/](https://vercel-status.com/)
- [https://twitter.com/vercel_status](https://twitter.com/vercel_status?lang=en)

If you believe there is a problem that has not been discovered yet, please contact us at [https://vercel.com/contact](https://vercel.com/contact) providing as much information as possible about the issue.
